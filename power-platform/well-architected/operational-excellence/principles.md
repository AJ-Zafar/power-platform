# Operational Excellence design principles 

The **design principles** are intended to provide guidance for aspects of Operational Excellence that you should consider throughout the development lifecycle. Start with the recommended approaches and **justify the benefits for a set of requirements**. After you set your strategy, drive actions by using the [**Operational Excellence checklist**](./checklist.md).
